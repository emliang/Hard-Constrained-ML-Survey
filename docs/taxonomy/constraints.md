
# Constraint Families

The repository tracks constraints at a level useful for comparing methods rather than deriving every mathematical detail.

## Continuous constraints

- Boxes and bounds
- Affine equality or inequality constraints
- Cones and semidefinite constraints
- General convex sets
- Nonlinear equalities and inequalities
- Manifolds and geometric domains

## Discrete or symbolic constraints

- Cardinality and sparsity constraints
- Logical constraints
- Grammar and automata constraints
- Integer or mixed-integer constraints

## Instance-dependent constraints

Some methods target feasible sets that change with an input, conditioning variable, design context, or sampling state. These require separate reporting because the oracle, projection, map, or certificate may be valid only under instance-specific assumptions.
